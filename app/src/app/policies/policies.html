SECTION 1 - A MEMBER OF THE BRITISH EMPIRE

The ECAU:

1.1 PROUDLY AFFIRMS that Australia is a colony of the british monarchy, established by the European race 1.2 AFFIRMS that Australia has the right to determine who crosses it's borders and who is eligible for citizenship 1.3 CALLS FOR a requirement that an individual must be indigenous to either Australia or a western aligned english speaking nation holding Australian citizenship if born abroad to be elected as a public official

SECTION 2 - SOCIETY AND CULTURE

The ECAU:

2.1 ENCOURAGES the complete freedom of speech and debate on topics such as culture, ethnicity and religion in the senate, house of representatives, university campuses and other public forums 2.2 CALLS ON public figures to encourage the unification of a monolithic Australian culture 2.3 CALLS FOR a permanent halt to immigration from third world, non catholic socieities and nations 2.4 CALLS FOR a permanent halt to third world and islamic refugees from Asia Minor and Africa 2.5 DISCOURAGES refugee intake encouraged by the United Nations or other international foreign organisations or non government organisations 2.6 ENCOURAGES the removal of ethnic diversity as a policy for immigration and society 2.7 ENCOURAGES the role of traditional motherhood as a legitmate institution for women with priority over careers 2.8 DISCOURAGES the advertising of sexually explicit material, including LGBT advertisements intended to target alternate sexualities

SECTION 3 - EDUCATION

The ECAU:

3.1 ENCOURAGES the removal of second, third, fourth and fifth wave feminism from the school cirriculum 3.2 CALLS FOR the banning of Marxism, Islamism or any radicalised ideology which oversimplifies the nature of a civilizaiton into an idealised world in school cirriculums 3.3 CALLS FOR the banning of the viewing of history through the lens of critical theory, critical race theory, or a postmodern neo-marxist agenda in school cirriculums 3.4 ENCOURAGES the teaching of the protestant faith as a nondogmatic understanding of our culture, without the profession of absolute faith in an omnipotent god 3.5 CALLS FOR the banning of teaching gender fluidity, intersectionality, intersex theories and LGBT material to under 18s

SECTION 4 - RELATIONS

The ECAU:

4.1 DISCOURAGES spending of foreign aid to non western allied nations by the state 4.2 DISCOURAGES permanent residency and citizenship rights for the children of those permanent resident workers 4.3 DISCOURAGES sharing of military technology or training with/of non western aligned countries soldiers 4.4 ENCOURAGES the use of tax money collected to be spent on national industries or as social welfare programs to decrease the presence of the lower class

SECTION 5 - RIGHTS

The ECAU:

5.1 DISCOURAGES the notion of international universal human rights pursued by Australia 5.2 ENCOURAGES the respect of the dignity of the individual for Australian citizens and the pursuit of indivdual liberty where it does not intrench on anothers dignity 5.3 AFFIRMS that anyone without a criminal record may have the right to visit Australia as a tourist or enter Australia for temporary business relations

SECTION 6 - LAW

The ECAU:

6.1 ENCOURAGES the removal of 18C racial vilification laws
